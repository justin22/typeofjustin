*noun*


Coercion is when someone forces or pressures another person to do something against their will. It's like making someone do stuff by using threats, intimidation, or other forceful tactics.


<TextToSpeech text='coercion' />

### Usage

1. The bully used coercion to make the smaller kids give him their lunch money every day.

2. The criminal resorted to coercion, threatening harm to force the witness into silence.

3. In some situations, people may comply out of fear, driven by coercion rather than genuine agreement
