*adjective*


friendly, pleasant, or good-natured. It's a way to describe someone who is easy to get along with and generally likable.



<TextToSpeech text='amiable' />


### Usage

1. Despite the stressful situation, Lisa remained amiable and greeted everyone with a warm smile.

2. The amiable neighbor always had a friendly wave and a willingness to help others in the community.

3. Jack's amiable personality made him popular at work, as colleagues appreciated his cheerful and approachable demeanor.
