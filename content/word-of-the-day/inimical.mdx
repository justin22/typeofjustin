*adjective*


Inimical means harmful or unfriendly, like something that is likely to cause damage or be hostile. It's used to describe situations, attitudes, or things that are not favorable and might work against you.



<TextToSpeech text='inimical' />


### Usage

1. The dark clouds and thunder indicated an inimical weather forecast, suggesting an impending storm.

2. The harsh criticism from the manager created an inimical work environment, making employees feel uneasy and stressed.

3. The invasive species proved inimical to the local ecosystem, causing a decline in native plant and animal populations.
