*adjective*


extremely difficult, tough, or unpleasant. It's often used to describe something that is exceptionally challenging or harsh.


<TextToSpeech text='hellacious' />

### Usage

1. The traffic during rush hour was hellacious, with cars bumper to bumper and honking echoing through the streets.

2. The storm brought a hellacious downpour, flooding the streets and causing chaos in the city.

3. Despite their best efforts, the hikers found themselves on a hellacious trail, facing steep slopes and treacherous terrain.
