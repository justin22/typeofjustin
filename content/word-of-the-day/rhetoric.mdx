*noun*

Rhetoric is like a tool or skill in using words to talk or write in a way that convinces or persuades others. It's about choosing the right words, organizing them well, and sometimes using tricks to make your message more effective. So, when someone is good at rhetoric, they can speak or write in a way that makes people listen, understand, and maybe even agree with them.

<TextToSpeech text='rhetoric' />

### Usage

1. The student used clever rhetoric to convince his friends to join the school club.
2. During the debate, her clear rhetoric helped everyone understand the complex issue.
3. The advertisement's powerful rhetoric persuaded customers to try the new product.
